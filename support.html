Which cameras are compatible?

Camera Assistant supports 33 Canon cameras including Cinema EOS (C70, C400), RF series (R5, R5 C, R6, R7), and DSLR (5D, 6D, 1D X). The EOS R5 C has full LCD screen support.

How does LCD analysis work?

Simply take a photo of your camera's LCD screen. Our AI analyzes the image and provides explanations, tips, and solutions for what's displayed.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, an internet connection is required for LCD analysis and search features. Favorites and history work offline.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Images are processed securely and not stored permanently. Your history and favorites stay on your device only.

Is this app affiliated with Canon?

No, Camera Assistant is an independent app. Canon, EOS and Canon product names are registered trademarks of Canon Inc.
